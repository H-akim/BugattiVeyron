Bugatti is a French company for the manufacture of cars and some aircraft parts, established in 1909 by an Italian man called Ettore Bugatti in the city of Molsheim in northern France.Bugatti was originally a fast car maker, but it failed at the beginning of World War II like other companies at that time, and the company fell into financial hardship, so it released its last model in 1950, and turned to the aircraft industry, and today it is owned by the Volkswagen Group and manufactures many cars Like the Bugatti Veyron.
